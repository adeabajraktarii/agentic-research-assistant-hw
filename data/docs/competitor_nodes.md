# Competitor Notes – Positioning

**Updated:** Week 12  
**Prepared by:** Product Team

## Competitor A: DataVista
- **Positioning:** “Low cost analytics for small teams”
- **Strengths:** Cheapest pricing, quick setup, simple dashboards
- **Weaknesses:** Limited customization, fewer integrations, basic security controls
- **Sales angle against us:** Discounts + fast procurement

## Competitor B: InsightLoop
- **Positioning:** “AI-first analytics and predictive insights”
- **Strengths:** Advanced AI features, strong marketing, polished UI
- **Weaknesses:** Higher licensing cost, long onboarding, rigid data model
- **Sales angle against us:** “We’re more advanced—pay more, get AI.”

## Our Draft Positioning
- **Balanced value:** fast deployment + flexible integrations + dependable support
- **Differentiator (near-term):** Analytics dashboard MVP (Week 16)
- **Differentiator (mid-term):** AI recommendations (Week 24)
- **Messaging:** “Get actionable analytics without long onboarding or vendor lock-in.”
