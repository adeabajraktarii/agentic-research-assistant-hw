# Pricing & Packaging Notes

**Prepared by:** Product  
**Updated:** Week 12

## Current Pricing Model
- Tiered pricing by number of users
- Annual contracts preferred
- Discounts offered case-by-case

## Competitive Pressure
- Competitor A offers aggressive discounts
- Competitor B charges premium for AI features

## Identified Risks
- Pricing sensitivity in mid-market segment
- Discounts impacting perceived value

## Mitigation Strategies
- Emphasize ROI and speed-to-value
- Bundle analytics features in higher tiers
- Limit discounts to annual plans only
